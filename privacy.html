Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Introduction

Carquinez, Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Chateau Archive Player mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Don't Collect

The Chateau Archive Player app is designed to be simple and privacy-focused. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Use analytics or tracking technologies
  • Require user registration or login
  • Store any user data on our servers
  • Share any data with third parties

Local Data Storage

The Chateau Archive Player app stores all data locally on your device. This includes:

  • Your audio files and playlists
  • App preferences and settings
  • Playback history

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

App Permissions

The Chateau Archive Player app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Storage access - to read and play your audio files
  • Internet access - to download audio files from archive.org

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and are not used to collect any personal information.

Interaction with Archive.org

The Chateau Archive Player app accesses audio content from archive.org in accordance with their terms of service and privacy policy. When you use the app to access content from archive.org:

  • The app makes standard HTTP requests to archive.org to retrieve audio files
  • These requests are subject to archive.org's standard privacy practices and terms of service
  • We recommend reviewing archive.org's privacy policy for more information about how they handle data

We do not modify or intercept any data transmitted between your device and archive.org.

Third-Party Services

Our app interacts with archive.org to provide access to audio content. This is the only third-party service the app uses, and it is essential for the app's core functionality. We do not integrate with any other third-party services that would collect user data.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at chateau.carquinez@gmail.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
